                            Men's Jumpers

                            Discover the Calvin Klein collection of men's jumpers. Classic colours. Striking textures. The finest of fabrics. Make a sophisticated statement with the CK collection of jumpers for men. Pair a roll-neck jumper with a blazer and trousers for a professional office outfit. Layer a knitted jumper under a jacket for added warmth. Or opt for a half-zip jumper as a sporty alternative. Pair it with jeans or chinos for a relaxed yet polished look. The Calvin Klein collection of men's jumpers is made with natural materials including wool and organic cotton. This makes them breathable, durable and gentle to the skin, so you can enjoy them for the rest of your life. Calvin Klein men’s jumpers. Knitwear with an edge.

                            How to wash a woollen jumper

                            To wash a woollen jumper, use cold water and a mild wool detergent. Gently hand wash the jumper, avoiding wringing or stretching it. Lay it flat on a towel to air dry, reshaping it to its original dimensions to maintain its shape and quality.

                            How to fold a jumper

                            To fold a jumper, lay it flat on a clean, dry surface and smooth out any wrinkles. Fold the jumper in half vertically, bringing one shoulder to meet the other. Then fold it horizontally, tucking the sleeves in for a neat and compact fold.
